Our Story

Started in a garage.
Still feels like one.

Pear started in 2019 with a single question: why does great technology have to feel so cold? We wanted to make products that felt like they were designed by someone who actually uses them.

Five years later, we're still small on purpose. Every product is reviewed by the same five people before it ships. We haven't lost that yet, and we plan to never lose it.

History

How we got here

2019

The Garage

Three friends, one rented garage, and a question: what if tech felt human? First prototype of Pear Buds built on a kitchen table.

2020

First 100 Orders

We posted on one forum. Sold out in 4 hours. Packed every box by hand. Still remember each order number.

2021

Pear Watch

Spent 18 months on a single product. Launched to a waiting list of 40,000 people. Shipped to 80 countries in the first week.

2023

Carbon Neutral

Every product we've ever shipped, offset. Going forward, every product will be carbon negative before it leaves the warehouse.

2025

Still Going

142,000 customers. 18 products. Same five people reviewing everything before it ships. Nothing has changed that matters.

What We Believe

The principles that drive us

01

Honest Design

Form follows function, always. We never add something because it looks cool. We add it because it works.

02

Made to Last

The most sustainable product is the one you don't need to replace. We build for decades, not product cycles.

03

Radical Transparency

Our margins are public. Our manufacturing partners are named. Our mistakes are on our blog. No hiding here.

04

Small on Purpose

We could scale faster. We choose not to. Size is the enemy of taste, and taste is all we have.
